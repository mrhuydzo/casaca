    • US STORY

    In Italian, Casa means House, and Ca is the abbreviation of Carpentería which means Carpenter. Hence, we took the name CasaCa as the meaning: the House of the Carpenter or briefly, the Carpenter’s House. We're a small company made up of three carpenters, based just outside Hanoi in a beautiful workshop space in the filed. We specialise in hand crafting industrial style furniture from wood, fabric and iron. We making furniture with creativity, enthusiasm, and ingenuity. In addition. After doing some researches, we recognize that the more minimalist we live, the happier we are. We want to make your home a gentle space, so you can slowly enjoy yourself by every moment, every day and every season. Every single one of your orders is packed and dispatched with love!
